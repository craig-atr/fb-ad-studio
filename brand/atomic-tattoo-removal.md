# Brand Profile — Atomic Tattoo Removal

The default brand for this project. Stages read this for offer, voice, audience,
and assets. To add another client later, copy this file and swap the values.

## Business

- **Name:** Atomic Tattoo Removal
- **Service:** Laser tattoo removal
- **Location / service area:** Wrentham, MA area; ~15–20 mile radius
- **Stage:** Newer studio. **Bookings are the priority metric.**
- **Website / landing:** _<add URL>_ — the landing page must repeat the ad's hook.

## Primary objective

Drive **free consultation bookings**. Meta campaign objective: **Leads** (pixel
verified and firing), not Traffic.

## Offer (default)

- **Free Consultation + $50 Off First Package**, with
- **Limited seasonal availability** for urgency.
- Seasonal variant (Mother's Day): "Mother's Day Package Specials Available."
- Avoid heavy discount language — it weakens perceived value. Frame as
  **Gift + Confidence + Fresh Start**.

## Audience

- Age 25–55.
- **Women first** for gifting/relational angles (e.g. Mother's Day), then test
  broader.
- Interests: tattoos, tattoo shops, tattoo aftercare, beauty/skincare, medical
  spa, aesthetics, permanent makeup correction.
- Retargeting (highest ROI): pricing-page and consultation-page visitors, IG/FB
  engagers, video viewers.

## Voice

- Playful, clever, emotionally relatable. Humor that's **light, never insulting
  or judgmental**. ("Mom never liked that tattoo anyway" — clever and shareable,
  not "Mom hates your tattoo.")
- Local and authentic-professional, not overproduced-commercial.
- Confident and premium; not a discount shop.

## CTA

- Always **Book Now** (not Learn More).

## Creative defaults

- **Format:** 9:16 vertical, 1080×1920, ~15–20 s (see
  `reference/facebook-ad-specs.md`).
- **Winning structure:** AI hook → real laser footage → real before/after →
  strong CTA + brand close.
- **Real footage the human supplies:** laser in action (pulses, frosting
  response), real before/after fading examples, real treatment room, logo.
- **AI scenes:** hook (person eyeing an old tattoo), emotional beat (the "mom
  look"), lifestyle/fresh-start, brand close environment.

## Compliance

- Medical/aesthetic advertising: **no guaranteed-results claims.** Say removal
  "can help," not "will erase."
- Before/after must be **real** and represent actual outcomes. Never AI-generate
  fake clinical results or fake before/after.
